## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with the best AI image generation experience:

### 1. User Input Data

   - **What We Collect**: Text prompts, descriptions, and instructions you provide to generate images; negative prompts; aspect ratio preferences; and generation settings.
   - **Purpose**: To process your requests through our AI models and generate custom images according to your specifications.
   - **Retention**: Prompts may be temporarily stored for processing and service improvement purposes.

### 2. Generated Content

   - **What We Collect**: AI-generated images created through our service based on your prompts.
   - **Purpose**: To deliver the generated images to you and improve our AI models' performance.
   - **User Rights**: You retain rights to images you generate, subject to our Terms of Service.

### 3. Account Information (Optional)

   - **What We Collect**: Email address and any optional profile information you choose to provide.
   - **Purpose**: To provide personalized services, save your preferences, and offer customer support.
   - **Note**: Account creation is optional for basic image generation features.

### 4. Usage Analytics

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you interact with our service, including features used, generation frequency, popular prompts (anonymized), and session duration.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our AI models, and enhance service performance.

### 5. Technical Information

   - **What We Collect**: Device type, browser information, IP address, and system specifications that may affect image generation performance.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our service for different devices and ensure compatibility with various systems.

### 6. Cookies and Local Storage

   - **What We Collect**: Preferences, session data, and temporary generation history stored locally on your device.
   - **Purpose**: To maintain your session, remember your settings, and provide a seamless user experience.
